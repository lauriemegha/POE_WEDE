About Us

The artist behind BADDIES2BADDIES

Prisselda Voltaire, mostly known as Amber is the owner of this luxury hairstyling brand that is BADDIES2BADDIES. She is an international master braider specialised in stitch braids, fulani, bohemian braids, and editorial hairstyles. With 6 years of practice and experience across Montreal-Canada, New York City-USA, and now Cape Town-South Africa, she aligns herself with the newest techniques to ensure you leave satisfied with her services.
